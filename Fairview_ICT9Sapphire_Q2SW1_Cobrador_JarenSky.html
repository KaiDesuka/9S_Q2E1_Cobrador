Siberian Husky Siberian Huskies are very small when they are still young but when they get older they grow much bigger. This dog breed needs a lot of attention, space for activities, time for walks, and a lot of effort to clean and brush their fur from how much they shed. This dog breed is one of the prettiest but also of the noisiest. I would love to have this dog
Toy Poodle Toy poodles can be easily trained due to their high intelligence. This dog breed is one of the begginer friendly dog breeds for new owners.
Shih tzu Shih tzus are very good dogs to have if you want a dog. They are small and if groomed well and correctly can have very beutiful fur Shih tzus are one of the best dogs to have even if your a new dog owner or an experienced one.
